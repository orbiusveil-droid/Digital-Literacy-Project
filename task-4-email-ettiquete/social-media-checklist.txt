Do’s ✅
	Don’ts ❌
	Think before you post and consider your reputation
	Share personal or sensitive information
	Use privacy settings to control visibility
	Post inappropriate or offensive content
	Keep profiles professional (especially for networking)
	Engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
	Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
	Overshare your daily life or location
	Give credit when sharing others’ content
	Post when emotional or upset
	Be mindful of your digital footprint
	Assume posts are completely private
